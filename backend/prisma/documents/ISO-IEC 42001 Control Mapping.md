# ISO/IEC 42001 Control Mapping

## 1. Purpose

This document provides a structured mapping between the requirements of ISO/IEC 42001 and the governance documentation that forms the Artificial Intelligence Management System operated by the organisation.

The mapping demonstrates how the organisation satisfies the governance requirements of the standard through documented policies, procedures, and operational controls.

This document is intended to support internal governance review and external certification audit preparation.

---

## 2. Scope

The mapping applies to the Artificial Intelligence Management System governing the design, deployment, and use of artificial intelligence systems within the organisation.

The document covers the core clauses of ISO/IEC 42001 and identifies the internal documents that address each requirement.

## 4. Supporting Governance Artefacts

The Artificial Intelligence Management System is supported by additional technical governance documentation that provides operational controls for AI systems.

Examples include:

- AI architectural standards
- data governance and classification standards
- audit trail and decision logging specifications
- data quality governance frameworks
- operational cost governance standards for AI systems

These artefacts provide the technical controls that support the governance procedures defined in the Artificial Intelligence Management System.

---

## 5. Maintenance of the Mapping

This document should be updated whenever:

- governance documentation changes
- new procedures are introduced
- the scope of the Artificial Intelligence Management System expands

Maintaining the mapping ensures that the organisation can demonstrate continued alignment with ISO/IEC 42001.
