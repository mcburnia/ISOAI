# Internal Audit and Management Review Procedure

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the procedure used by Gibbs Consulting to review the effectiveness of the Artificial Intelligence Management System.

Periodic review ensures that governance procedures remain appropriate, that risks are being managed effectively, and that the organisation continues to meet professional and regulatory expectations relating to artificial intelligence systems.

This procedure forms part of the Artificial Intelligence Management System established in alignment with ISO/IEC 42001.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to the governance processes that make up the Artificial Intelligence Management System.

The review covers:

- governance policies
- operational procedures
- risk management activities
- system lifecycle governance
- monitoring and incident management processes

The purpose of the review is to confirm that the system continues to operate effectively.

---

## 3. Internal Audit

Internal audit provides an independent review of whether governance procedures are operating as intended.

Internal audit activities may include examination of:

- AI system inventory records
- risk register entries
- lifecycle approval records
- incident investigation documentation
- monitoring and oversight activities

Internal audit is not intended to assign fault. Its purpose is to assess whether governance procedures are functioning effectively and consistently.

---

## 4. Audit Frequency

Internal audit of the Artificial Intelligence Management System should occur periodically.

Audit may be triggered by:

- scheduled governance review
- introduction of new AI systems
- significant incidents or operational concerns
- organisational governance review cycles

The frequency of audit should remain proportionate to the scale of AI activity within the organisation.

---

## 5. Audit Findings

Internal audit may identify several types of findings.

These may include:

- governance procedures operating effectively
- opportunities to improve documentation or process clarity
- areas where procedures are not being followed consistently
- potential governance risks requiring attention

Findings should be documented and reviewed by the AI Governance Lead.

---

## 6. Corrective Actions

Where audit findings identify weaknesses or governance gaps, corrective actions should be defined.

Corrective actions may include:

- updating governance documentation
- improving monitoring procedures
- clarifying responsibilities
- providing additional training or guidance

Corrective actions should be tracked until they are resolved.

---

## 7. Management Review

Senior leadership should periodically review the operation of the Artificial Intelligence Management System.

Management review ensures that leadership maintains visibility over AI governance and associated risks.

Management review may consider:

- summary of AI system activity
- significant risks recorded in the risk register
- incidents involving AI systems
- findings from internal audit
- opportunities to improve governance practices

The purpose of management review is to confirm that the Artificial Intelligence Management System remains appropriate and effective.

---

## 8. Documentation of Review

Internal audits and management reviews should be documented.

Documentation may include:

- audit observations
- improvement actions
- governance decisions arising from review
- updates to governance procedures

These records demonstrate that the organisation actively maintains and improves its governance framework.

---

## 9. Continuous Improvement

The Artificial Intelligence Management System should evolve in response to:

- operational experience with AI systems
- developments in regulatory guidance
- lessons identified from incidents
- technological developments affecting AI governance

Continuous improvement ensures that governance practices remain aligned with emerging expectations.

---

## 10. Ownership and Review

Document Owner: AI Governance Lead  
Review Cycle: Annual  
Approval Authority: Senior Management
